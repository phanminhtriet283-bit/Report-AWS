# Launch of the AWS Asia Pacific (New Zealand) Region

Today, we are excited to announce the availability of the **AWS Asia Pacific (New Zealand) Region – ap-southeast-6**.  
This new region enables customers to deploy workloads and store data within New Zealand, delivering **lower latency** and supporting organizations with local data residency and regulatory requirements.

![Figure 1: AWS Asia Pacific (New Zealand) Region](images/hinh1.png)

**Figure 1: AWS Asia Pacific (New Zealand) Region**

---

## Why this region matters

The new region provides customers an additional option to deploy applications closer to end users in New Zealand. This is especially important for industries such as:

- financial services,  
- government,  
- healthcare,  
- media & telecommunications,  
- and organizations requiring strong data sovereignty.

The region improves:

- latency,  
- availability,  
- durability,  
- and user experience across the APAC area.

---

## Infrastructure design of the New Zealand Region

The AWS Asia Pacific (New Zealand) Region includes:

- **3 Availability Zones (AZs)**  
- multiple layers of security controls  
- high-performance, low-latency networking

Each Availability Zone is located in a separate geographic area and is designed to be:

- physically isolated,  
- yet interconnected for **high availability**  
- and fault-tolerant architectures.

## Accelerating AI & Generative AI adoption

This new region empowers organizations to run AI/ML workloads locally, including:

- Amazon Bedrock,  
- Amazon SageMaker,  
- real-time inference with lower latency,  
- secure training and deployment under data residency requirements.

AWS emphasizes building AI systems that are sustainable, secure, and responsible.

---

## Commitment to sustainability

AWS aims to:

- power its operations with **100% renewable energy by 2025**,  
- move toward carbon neutrality,  
- and design data centers optimized for energy efficiency.

Data centers in New Zealand follow AWS’s sustainability best practices, helping customers reduce the energy footprint of their cloud workloads.

---

## Expanded options for customers

With this new region, customers can:

- build multi-region architectures,  
- implement **disaster recovery (DR)** and **business continuity (BCP)** plans,  
- use multi-AZ replication strategies,  
- and increase reliability and fault tolerance.

AWS continues to support customers in innovating faster, operating securely, and scaling globally.

# Conclusion

The launch of the **AWS Asia Pacific (New Zealand) Region** marks an important milestone in AWS’s long-term commitment to the Asia-Pacific region.

This region provides:

- enhanced support for AI/ML workloads,  
- and a secure foundation for business growth and innovation.
